About Us

Scholarship Sports USA

assists and supports talented, passionate footballers who wish to advance their careers and graduate with a bachelor’s degree, all the while living in the United States of America. There are over 2,000 US Universities that offer athletic football (soccer) scholarships, so finding the right college for you, is the easy part! If you’re prepared to put in the hours on the training ground and in the classroom, then America is the right destination for you.

Imagine waking up in New York, California, or Florida. SSU can make that happen for you. The US College system is heavily funded and the standard of football is tremendously high. Signing for SSU will give you the best possible platform to make an impact in the United States. Whether you are looking at turning professional or simply looking to continue playing at an ever-increasing standard whilst gaining your bachelor’s degree, then SSU is the only choice for you.
